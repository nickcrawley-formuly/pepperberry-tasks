# Pepperberry — User Guide

---

## Logging In

![Screenshot: Login screen](images/login.png)

1. Open the app in your browser
2. You'll see the **Private and Confidential** login screen
3. Tap **"Login as.."** and select your name from the dropdown
4. Four PIN boxes will appear — enter your 4-digit PIN
5. The app will log you in automatically after the 4th digit

**First time logging in?** You'll be asked to set a new 4-digit PIN. Choose something you'll remember — this is your password from now on.

**Forgot your PIN?** Ask Nick or Anna to reset it for you in User Management.

## Dashboard

![Screenshot: Dashboard with nav buttons and job list](images/dashboard.png)

The dashboard is your home screen. At the top you'll see:
- Your **name** and **session timer** ("Session expires in Xh Xm")
- A **logout icon** (door with arrow) to sign out
- **Navigation buttons** to switch between sections

### Navigation Buttons

![Screenshot: Navigation button row](images/nav-buttons.png)

| Button | Who sees it | What it does |
|--------|-------------|--------------|
| **New Job** | Admin only | Create a new job |
| **Jobs** | Everyone | View the job board |
| **Weather** | Admin + permitted users | Local weather and rainfall |
| **Cart** | Admin + permitted users | Shared shopping list |
| **Users** | Admin only | Manage user accounts |

### Job List

![Screenshot: Job list with filters](images/job-list.png)

- Jobs are listed with their **title**, **status**, **priority**, and **who it's assigned to**
- **Urgent jobs** always appear at the top
- Use the filter buttons to narrow by status, priority, or category
- Admins can also filter by assigned user

## Viewing a Job

![Screenshot: Job detail page](images/job-detail.png)

Tap any job to see its full details:
- **Title** and **description**
- **Status** (To Do / In Progress / Done)
- **Priority**, **category**, and **location**
- **Due date** (if set)
- **Who it's assigned to**

### Updating Status

![Screenshot: Status update buttons](images/status-update.png)

- Tap the status button to move a job forward: **To Do → In Progress → Done**
- Only update jobs assigned to you (admins can update any job)

### Adding Comments

![Screenshot: Comment section](images/comments.png)

- Scroll down to the comments section
- Type your comment and tap **Post**
- Comments show who posted and when

### Adding Photos

![Screenshot: Photo section with upload](images/photos.png)

- Tap **Add Photo** to upload an image from your phone
- Photos are compressed automatically before uploading
- Maximum **5 photos** per job
- Both admins and tradies can add photos to their jobs

## Creating a Job (Admin Only)

![Screenshot: New job form](images/new-job.png)

1. Tap **New Job** on the dashboard
2. Fill in the details:
   - **Title** — short description of the work
   - **Description** — detailed notes (optional)
   - **Priority** — Low, Medium, High, or Urgent
   - **Category** — Maintenance, Horses, Donkeys, Fencing, Regal Riding, or General
   - **Location** — where on the property
   - **Assigned to** — which tradie or staff member
   - **Due date** — when it needs to be done (optional)
3. For recurring jobs, set a **repeat schedule** (daily, weekly, fortnightly, or monthly)
4. Tap **Create Job**

---

## Editing a Job (Admin Only)

![Screenshot: Edit job form](images/edit-job.png)

1. Open a job and tap **Edit**
2. Change any details as needed
3. Tap **Save**

You can also:
- **Transfer** a job to a different person
- **Delete** a single job or an entire recurring series

## Weather

![Screenshot: Weather page - current conditions](images/weather-current.png)

The weather page shows conditions for the Pepperberry area using Open-Meteo data.

### Current Conditions

- **Temperature** (highlighted red when 30°C or above)
- A one-sentence **summary** with advice for outdoor work
- **Stats row** with icons: humidity, wind speed, rainfall, and sea temperature at Kiama

### 7-Day Forecast

![Screenshot: 7-day forecast table](images/weather-forecast.png)

- Each day shows the **weather icon**, **description**, **high/low temps**, **rain probability %**, and **expected rainfall**

### Rainfall Chart

![Screenshot: 30-day rainfall chart](images/weather-rainfall.png)

- **30-day bar chart** comparing this year (dark blue) with last year (light blue)
- Tap any bar for exact figures
- A **"Today"** marker shows where you are in the timeline

### Rain Radar

![Screenshot: Rain radar placeholder and loaded](images/weather-radar.png)

- Shows a **play button** by default to save data
- **Tap or hover** to load the live Windy.com radar for the Wollongong region

### Year-to-Date Rainfall

![Screenshot: YTD rainfall comparison](images/weather-ytd.png)

- Total rainfall this year vs last year
- Monthly breakdown underneath

## Shopping List

![Screenshot: Shopping list page](images/shopping.png)

A shared list for things that need buying.

### Adding an Item

![Screenshot: Add shopping item form](images/shopping-add.png)

1. Type the **item name**
2. Select a **category** (Hardware, Hay, Feed, or Other)
3. Choose **who's buying it** (Nick or Anna)
4. Tap **Add**

### Managing Items

- Each item shows **who added it** and **who's assigned to buy it**
- Tap the **tick** to mark as bought
- Tap the **bin** to delete

## User Management (Admin Only)

![Screenshot: User management page](images/users.png)

### Adding a New User

![Screenshot: Add user form](images/users-add.png)

1. Tap **Add User**
2. Enter their **name**
3. Select a **role**:
   - **Admin** — full access to everything
   - **Tradie** — sees only their assigned jobs
   - **Regal Riding** — sees only Regal Riding tasks
4. For tradies, select their **trade type** (fencer, plumber, electrician, handyman, landscaper, housekeeper, general, animal carer)
5. Optionally grant access to extra sections (Weather, Cart)
6. Tap **Create**

The new user will be prompted to set their own PIN on first login.

### Editing a User

![Screenshot: Edit user form](images/users-edit.png)

- Tap a user to edit their details
- You can change their role, trade type, or section access
- **Deactivate** a user to prevent them from logging in (without deleting them)
- **Reset PIN** to force them to set a new one on next login

---

## Tips

- **Sessions last 3 hours** — after that you'll need to log in again
- **Works on any device** — designed for phones, but works on tablets and desktops too
- **Photos are compressed** automatically so they upload quickly even on slow connections
- **Urgent jobs** always float to the top of the list so nothing gets missed
