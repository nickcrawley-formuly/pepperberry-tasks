# Pepperberry — Guide for Tradies & Staff

---

## Logging In

![Screenshot: Login screen](images/login.png)

1. Open the app link in your phone browser
2. Tap **"Login as.."** and select your name
3. Enter your **4-digit PIN**
4. You'll be logged in automatically after the 4th digit

**First time?** You'll be asked to choose a 4-digit PIN. This is your password from now on — pick something you'll remember.

**Forgot your PIN?** Contact Nick or Anna and they'll reset it for you.

---

## Your Job Board

![Screenshot: Dashboard with job list](images/tradie-dashboard.png)

After logging in you'll see your **job board** — a list of all jobs assigned to you.

Each job card shows:
- **Title** — what needs doing
- **Priority** — Low, Medium, High, or Urgent
- **Location** — where on the property
- **Due date** — when it needs to be done (if set)
- **Status** — To Do, In Progress, or Done

**Urgent jobs** always appear at the top so you don't miss them.

You can filter your jobs by status using the buttons at the top of the list.

---

## Viewing a Job

![Screenshot: Job detail page](images/tradie-job-detail.png)

Tap any job to see the full details:
- **Description** — detailed notes about what's needed
- **Location** — where on the property
- **Priority** and **due date**
- **Photos** — reference images from the admin or previous work
- **Comments** — messages between you and the admin

---

## Updating Job Status

![Screenshot: Status buttons](images/tradie-status.png)

As you work through a job, update its status:

1. **To Do** — you haven't started yet
2. **In Progress** — you're working on it
3. **Done** — the work is finished

Just tap the status button to move it forward. The admin will be notified when you mark a job as done.

---

## Adding Photos

![Screenshot: Photo upload](images/tradie-photos.png)

You can add photos to show your progress or completed work:

1. Open a job
2. Scroll to the **Photos** section
3. Tap **Add Photo**
4. Take a photo or choose one from your gallery
5. The photo uploads automatically (it's compressed first so it works on slow connections)

You can add up to **5 photos** per job. This helps the admin see what's been done without visiting the site.

---

## Adding Comments

![Screenshot: Comment section](images/tradie-comments.png)

Need to ask a question or leave a note about a job?

1. Open the job
2. Scroll to the **Comments** section
3. Type your message
4. Tap **Post**

The admin will see your comment and can reply.

---

## Session & Logging Out

![Screenshot: Header with session timer and logout](images/tradie-header.png)

- Your session lasts **3 hours** — after that you'll need to log in again
- The time remaining shows in the header: **"Session expires in Xh Xm"**
- To log out early, tap the **door icon** in the top right corner

## Quick Reference

| What you can do | How |
|-----------------|-----|
| See your jobs | Open the app — they're on your dashboard |
| Update a job's status | Open the job → tap the status button |
| Add a photo | Open the job → scroll to Photos → tap Add Photo |
| Leave a comment | Open the job → scroll to Comments → type and tap Post |
| Filter your jobs | Use the filter buttons above the job list |
| Log out | Tap the door icon in the top right |

| What you can't do | Why |
|--------------------|-----|
| See other people's jobs | Each person only sees their own work |
| Create or delete jobs | Only admins can create and remove jobs |
| Reassign a job | Contact Nick or Anna if a job needs reassigning |
